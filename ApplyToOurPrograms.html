Apply To Our Programs

Everything you need to know about Valor Bridge, Journey, and Connect.

The Valor Bridge Program

Currently offered twice a year, with cohorts in the Spring (late March start) and Fall (late September start).

  • For more information, fill out our Expression Of Interest Form or contact Andrea Filz at andrea@salutecolorado.org or 303‑908‑1781.

The Valor Journey Program

This program runs annually with a new cohort in April.

  • For details, please complete our Expression Of Interest Form or contact Andrea Filz at andrea@salutecolorado.org or 303‑908‑1781.

The Valor Connect Program

Ongoing and open to everyone—participants, alumni, mentors, donors, and volunteers.

  • To receive event invites, please sign up for our newsletter.
