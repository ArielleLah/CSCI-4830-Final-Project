2025 Salute Colorado Expression of Interest Form

Please complete this form if you are interested in participating in either the Valor Bridge or Valor Journey programs. Brief program descriptions are included below:

  • Valor Bridge - consists of a comprehensive, in-person, 10-week curriculum led by executive coaches and leadership development professionals focusing on key components such as translating military skill sets, interviewing and salary negotiations, personality profiles, identifying strengths, business economics, leadership training and effective networking and relationship building. At the end of the program, fellows are paired with a mentor in their desired field. The ideal candidate for Valor Bridge is preparing to or recently separated from the Armed Services and looking for a more intensive training program to help move forward on their path to civilian sector success.
  • Valor Journey - a 2-year program that provides participants with a dedicated mentor, goal setting assistance, funding for professional development, invitations to networking events, training opportunities, and peer support to navigate their pursuit of post-service career objectives. The ideal candidate for Valor Journey is recently separated from the Armed Services with a focus on a specific education and career path that could be enhanced by the support of a mentor in that field.

We will respond to your inquiry within 2 business days.

You can also reach us at salutecolorado@gmail.com with any questions.
